Frequently Asked Questions *

What defines the Trendy % Terrace philosopy?

At Trendy & Terrace , our design philosopy revolves around crafting enduring spaces by blending classic aesthetics with modern simplicity. We believe in creating pieces that stand the test of time, both in style and functionality.

How do I care for my Trendy & Terrace furniture?

The first step towards cleaning is dusting or wiping with a soft cloth. Mild scrubbing with soap and water removes dirt from all furniture. Rinse well and dry completely before storing the furniture. Avoid the use of harsh chemicals as they may damage the material of the furniture.

Can I customize furniture to suit my unique style?

Finding the right furniture pieces that fit your style and area can be challenging when furnishing a space. But what if you could customize your furniture to suit your unique needs and preferences? That's where March Legend fits the bill.

What materials are used in Trendy & Terrace furniture?

Several types of material are used to produce furniture. The most commonly used materials include particleboard, MDF, plywood, veneer sheets, furniture board, and lumber. Once you get a clear picture of each material, it is easier to choose.
